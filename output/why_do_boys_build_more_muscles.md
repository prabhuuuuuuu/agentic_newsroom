# Why Do Boys Build More Muscles Than Girls?

As a technical writer, I've come across a common misconception in the fitness industry: the idea that boys build muscle faster than girls. However, research has shown that this is not entirely accurate. In this article, we'll delve into the reasons behind this misconception and explore the facts.

## Introduction

The notion that boys build muscle faster than girls has been a persistent myth in the fitness industry. However, recent research has challenged this idea, suggesting that both males and females can build muscle at similar rates. In this article, we'll examine the reasons behind this misconception and explore the science behind muscle growth in both males and females.

## Debunking the Myth: Males Do Not Build Muscle Faster than Females

One of the primary sources of this misconception is the idea that males have a greater potential to build absolute muscle mass. However, research has shown that when standardized as a percentage of their starting muscle mass, females can build muscle similarly to males (Befit Training Physio, 2023). This suggests that the myth of males building muscle faster than females may be overstated.

## Do Men and Women Build Muscle and Strength Differently?

A recent meta-analysis by Refalo et al (2025) found that while men gained more absolute muscle mass, there were virtually no differences in relative gains (Stronger By Science, 2025). This suggests that both males and females can achieve significant muscle growth and strength gains through consistent training, proper nutrition, and a well-designed exercise program.

## ELI5: Why Do Males Typically Build Muscle Easier Than Females?

The hormone testosterone plays a significant role in muscle growth. Males produce more testosterone than women, which can make it easier for them to build muscle (Reddit, n.d.). However, this doesn't mean that females can't build muscle at all. With the right training and nutrition, females can achieve significant muscle growth and strength gains.

## Men and Women May Build Muscle Differently Due to Factors Like...

Research has shown that factors like muscle fiber type and hormones can also play a role in muscle growth (FitStop Malta, n.d.). Males tend to have more muscle mass and higher testosterone, which can give them an advantage when it comes to building muscle. However, females can still build muscle with the right training and nutrition.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, the idea that boys build muscle faster than girls is a misconception. While males may have an advantage when it comes to building absolute muscle mass, females can still build muscle and achieve significant strength gains with the right training and nutrition. By understanding the science behind muscle growth and addressing any misconceptions, we can work towards creating a more inclusive and supportive fitness community for all.
